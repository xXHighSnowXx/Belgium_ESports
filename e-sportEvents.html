E-SPORTS

Detailed Game Descriptions & Formats

F1-2025

Solo

Come join us for high-speed, wheel-to-wheel racing in F1-2025.

F1-2025 is the latest official Formula 1 racing game. It delivers realistic physics, stunning graphics, and intense competition on official circuits worldwide. Perfect for players who love precision, strategy, and high-speed racing. Races can be run individually or as part of team championships.

Rocket League

Team-based (2v2 & 3v3)

Come join us for a thrilling 2v2 or 3v3 in Rocket League.

Rocket League combines soccer with rocket-powered cars in a fast-paced, skill-based arena. Teamwork, aerial control, and quick decision-making are essential. It is one of the most popular team esports games in the world, known for its high mechanical skill ceiling and exciting gameplay.

Mortal Kombat

1v1 (Solo)

Come join us for intense one-on-one battles in Mortal Kombat.

Mortal Kombat is a legendary fighting game franchise known for its brutal combat, deep character roster, and cinematic fatalities. It emphasizes one-on-one duels where timing, combos, and mind games determine the winner. Perfect for players who enjoy competitive fighting games.

League of Legends

Team-based (5v5)

Come join us for epic 5v5 battles in League of Legends.

League of Legends (LoL) is a strategic 5v5 MOBA where two teams battle to destroy the enemy base. It requires strong teamwork, macro strategy, individual skill, and coordination. One of the biggest esports titles globally with professional leagues around the world.

Marvel Rivals

Team-based (6v6)

Come join us for exciting 6v6 hero battles in Marvel Rivals.

Marvel Rivals is a fast-paced team-based hero shooter featuring iconic Marvel characters. Players work together in 6v6 matches, using unique abilities and ultimate skills to outplay the opposing team. It combines great visuals, hero variety, and chaotic team fights.

Call of Duty

Team-based (4v4 / 6v6)

Come join us for intense team battles in Call of Duty.

Call of Duty is a world-famous first-person shooter series known for its fast action, tactical gameplay, and competitive multiplayer modes. Matches are team-based, focusing on objectives, communication, and map control. Popular modes include Search & Destroy, Hardpoint, and Control.
